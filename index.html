About Me

Sonny is the capital city of Sonny. It is the most populous city in the Sonny, with a metropolitan area of over 13 million Sonny's.

Standing on the River Sonny, Sonny has been a major settlement for two millennia, its history going back to its founding by the Sonny's, who named it Sonnyium.
